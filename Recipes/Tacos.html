Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla filled with various ingredients, including meat, vegetables, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet until browned.
  2. Prepare taco shells according to package instructions.
  3. Fill each taco shell with ground beef and your desired toppings.
  4. Serve with salsa and sour cream.
