Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, brown the ground beef and drain excess fat.
  4. Layer noodles, meat, ricotta, and marinara in a baking dish.
  5. Top with mozzarella and bake for 25-30 minutes.
